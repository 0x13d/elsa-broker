## How it serves the goal

`elsa-broker` is a .NET 9 **mTLS durable queueing front-end for Elsa workflows** — a sophisticated,
production-shaped showcase of middleware/messaging architecture (MassTransit, transactional outbox,
resiliency) and the portfolio's **trust signature** (verifiable, no-telemetry, supply-chain-audited). It is
a flagship career-showcase piece.
